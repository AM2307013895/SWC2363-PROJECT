Our Services

Film Developing

We offer high-quality film developing services for all types of film, from 35mm to 120 and beyond. Our expert technicians use state-of-the-art equipment to ensure your film is developed to the highest standards.

Film Scanning

Our film scanning service allows you to digitize your film negatives or positives, preserving your memories for generations to come. We offer high-resolution scans with precise color correction and noise reduction.

Film Camera Service

Keep your film camera in top condition with our expert repair and maintenance services. Our skilled technicians can fix everything from minor issues to major overhauls, ensuring your camera is always ready to capture life's precious moments.


Get in Touch

For any inquiries regarding our services, you are welcome to contact us.

About Us

We are a Kuala Lumpur-based film lab specializing in film development, scanning, and offering a range of films for purchase. Whether you're capturing memories on disposable cameras or using professional film stocks, we're here to bring your moments to life.
